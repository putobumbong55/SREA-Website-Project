What Is Our Goal?

At SREA, our goal is to empower individuals, communities, and future generations with the knowledge and confidence to embrace clean, renewable energy. We are an eco-conscious association dedicated to raising awareness and understanding of hydro, solar, and wind energy as essential solutions for a sustainable future.

We aim to make renewable energy education accessible, practical, and engaging while helping people understand not only how these technologies work, but also how they can be applied in everyday life. Through workshops, resources, and community initiatives, we strive to inspire informed choices that reduce environmental impact and support a transition away from fossil fuels.

Ultimately, SREA exists to build a more sustainable world by encouraging learning, innovation, and collective action in renewable energy.

Wind Power

Wind turbines turn the wind's energy into electricity by spinning blades connected to a generator, providing clean, renewable power.

Solar Energy

Solar power is energy from the sun that is captured using solar panels and converted into electricity for homes, businesses, and other uses as a clean and renewable energy source.

Hydro Power

Hydro power is electricity generated by using the force of moving water, usually from rivers or dams, to spin turbines connected to generators, producing clean and renewable energy.
